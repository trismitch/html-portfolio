The Best Movies According to Tristan

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Midsommar

Ari Aster does a beautiful job with this film. The cinematography is beautiful and Florence Pugh does an amazing job acting. The writing is also beautiful!

Twilight

This movie gets a lot of hate, but I think it's a classic. Campy, romantic, and it has vampires, which is cool.

The Hunger Games

The OG YA dystopian movie. Amazing acting and the cinematography for the whole series is really amazing.
